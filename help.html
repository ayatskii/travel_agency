Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my travel dates?

Open your ticket in your account and click “Modify”. If you need help — contact us in chat.

What are the refund policies?

Conditions depend on your fare. Standard fare allows free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure

How can I add baggage?

On the booking page select “Baggage”, or request extra baggage via support chat.
