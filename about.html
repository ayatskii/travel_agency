About BlueWave Travel

Crafting unforgettable journeys since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by passionate travelers Altynay, Nuray, Aiat and Merey, BlueWave Travel began as a small boutique agency with a big dream: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone.

What started as a four-person operation planning custom itineraries from a cozy Miami office has grown into a team of 25 travel experts who have helped over 50,000 travelers discover the world's most beautiful destinations.

Our name "BlueWave" represents our love for ocean adventures and our commitment to riding the wave of innovation in travel experiences. We believe that every journey should be as unique as the traveler embarking on it, and we pour that philosophy into every itinerary we create.

Over the years, we've expanded from local Florida getaways to global adventures, but our core mission remains the same: to deliver exceptional service, personalized attention, and memories that last a lifetime.

Our Mission & Values

🌊 Our Mission

To create transformative travel experiences that inspire, educate, and connect people with the world's diverse cultures and natural wonders while maintaining the highest standards of service and sustainability.

We strive to be more than just a travel agency - we aim to be catalysts for personal growth and global understanding. Through carefully curated experiences, we help travelers step outside their comfort zones and discover new perspectives.

Our mission extends beyond the trip itself. We're committed to ensuring that every journey leaves a positive impact on both the traveler and the destinations we visit.

Our Commitment

Sustainable Tourism

We believe in responsible travel that benefits both travelers and local communities.

Our partnerships ensure that 5% of every booking goes toward environmental conservation and community development projects in the destinations we visit. We work with local guides, support family-owned businesses, and promote eco-friendly accommodations.

We're committed to reducing our carbon footprint through carbon offset programs and promoting sustainable travel practices among our clients.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist you during your travels.

From flight changes to local recommendations, we're here for you every step of the way. Our multilingual support team can assist with emergencies, itinerary changes, and any questions that arise during your journey.

We provide local emergency contacts in every destination and maintain relationships with service providers worldwide to ensure quick resolution of any issues.

Best Price Guarantee

We're committed to offering the best value for your money.

If you find a better price for the same package, we'll match it and give you an additional 10% discount. Our partnerships with airlines, hotels, and local providers allow us to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality.

We regularly monitor market prices and adjust our offerings to ensure you get the best possible deal for your dream vacation.
