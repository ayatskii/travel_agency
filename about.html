About BlueWave Travel

Crafting unforgettable journeys since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by passionate travelers Altynay, Nuray, Aiat and Merey, BlueWave Travel began as a small boutique agency with a big dream: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone.

What started as a four-person operation planning custom itineraries from a cozy Miami office has grown into a team of 25 travel experts who have helped over 50,000 travelers discover the world's most beautiful destinations.

Our name "BlueWave" represents our love for ocean adventures and our commitment to riding the wave of innovation in travel experiences.

Our Commitment

Sustainable Tourism

We believe in responsible travel that benefits both travelers and local communities. Our partnerships ensure that 5% of every booking goes toward environmental conservation and community development projects in the destinations we visit.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist you during your travels. From flight changes to local recommendations, we're here for you every step of the way.

Best Price Guarantee

We're committed to offering the best value for your money. If you find a better price for the same package, we'll match it and give you an additional 10% discount.
